Welcome to the HP-API!

Use these routes to request data on Harry Potter characters:

  • http://own-hp-api.herokuapp.com/api/characters - view all characters
  • http://own-hp-api.herokuapp.com/api/characters/students - view all characters who are Hogwarts students during the book series
  • http://own-hp-api.herokuapp.com/api/characters/staff - view all characters who are Hogwarts staff during the book series
  • http://own-hp-api.herokuapp.com/api/characters/house/:house - view all characters in a certain house, e.g. /slytherin

No API key is required.
